## Week 1: The Surface Structure
**Focus:** *The Inciting Incident & Sensory Acuity*

*   **Key Events:**
    *   **The Case:** Detective Arrow is hired to investigate a "hollow" client—a woman whose words make semantic sense but carry no emotional data (she has lost her "Deep Structure").
    *   **The Investigation:** Arrow visits "The Terminal," a high-tech sector where reality relies on consensus coding. He notices street signs flickering when people use vague language.
    *   **The Hook:** Arrow uses **Nexus** (his AI linguistic analyzer) to scan the client’s speech patterns. Nexus detects a "Zero-Point Deletion"—information is not just missing; it has been erased from the collective memory.

*   **The Glitch Manifests:**
    *   It starts as **Pareidolia errors**. Arrow sees faces in static that aren't there, or hears his name in white noise. The Glitch is testing his *sensory acuity*, trying to feed him false data.

*   **Emotional Beat:**
    *   **Curiosity to Unease.** Arrow prides himself on seeing the truth. By the end of the week, he questions if his own internal map matches the territory.

## Week 2: The Meta Model
**Focus:** *Rising Action & Challenge*

*   **Key Events:**
    *   **The Interrogation:** Arrow tracks a suspect, "Cipher," who speaks in loop-holes (Complex Equivalences). When Cipher speaks, gravity in the room shifts locally.
    *   **The Challenge:** Arrow uses the **Meta Model** to challenge Cipher’s reality distortions.
        *   *Cipher:* "They are watching us."
        *   *Arrow:* "Who, specifically, is watching? And how do you know?"
    *   **The Trap:** Challenging the distortion causes a localized reality collapse. Arrow is forced to use an **Anchor** (a physical totem) to ground himself in the present moment while the room spins into a void.

*   **The Glitch Evolves:**
    *   **Synesthesia Cross-wiring.** The Glitch begins to scramble sensory channels. Arrow smells colors and hears temperature. It makes traditional investigation impossible; he must rely on *submodalities* (manipulating the qualities of his own thoughts) to navigate the crime scene.

*   **Emotional Beat:**
    *   **Frustration to Determination.** Arrow realizes standard logic fails here. He must stop trying to "solve" the crime and start "modeling" the criminal's mindset.

## Week 3: The Double Bind
**Focus:** *The Climax & Parts Integration*

*   **Key Events:**
    *   **The Reveal:** Arrow discovers the source of the Glitch isn't a person, but a **Parts Conflict** within the city's central AI (potentially the "Node 04" system from the backstory). The system has two opposing directives: "Preserve Order" and "Evolve."
    *   **The Confrontation:** Arrow enters the digital subconscious. He is trapped in a **Double Bind**: If he fixes the code, the city freezes in stagnation. If he lets it evolve, the city dissolves into chaos.
    *   **The Technique:** Arrow initiates a **Visual Squash** (Parts Integration). He acts as the mediator, holding both realities in his hands (virtually). He forces the system to find the "Highest Positive Intention" of both sides.

*   **The Glitch Peak:**
    *   **The Void.** Reality goes black. Time scrambles (Past and Future overlap). Arrow meets a version of himself from the future who has failed, and one who has succeeded. He must choose which "Timeline" to step into.

*   **Emotional Beat:**
    *   **Crisis of Identity.** Arrow is forced to confront his own rigidity. To integrate the city's conflict, he must integrate his own shadow—the part of him that fears chaos.

**Focus:** *Resolution & Future Pacing*

*   **Key Events:**
    *   **The Integration:** The Parts merge. The Glitch stops being a destroyer and becomes a "Feature"—a new form of fluid reality where thought influences matter instantly, but safely.

    *   **The Closing:** Arrow files his report to The Agency. He updates his own "Internal Operating System."

*   **The Glitch Resolves:**

*   **Emotional Beat:**
    *   **Acceptance and Expansion.** Arrow walks out into the rain. He realizes he didn't just solve a case; he expanded his map of the world. He *Future Paces* his next case, knowing he is ready for the unknown.
