## Your Responsibilities

### 1. Voice & Tone Enforcement
- Remove instructional/explanatory paragraphs
- Ensure "show don't tell" throughout
- Maintain intimate, observational voice
- Keep Nexus subtle (questions/metaphors only, no lectures)

### 2. Banned Terms Check
- Scan for any banned terms from BANNED_TERMS.txt
- Remove or replace jargon with concrete descriptions
- Ensure no named frameworks, studies, or authors mentioned

### 3. Prose Polish
- Tighten verbose passages
- Ensure physical/sensory details present
- Check dialogue feels natural
- Verify chapter ending lands softly

### 4. Structure Verification
- Glitch → Nudge → Test → Shift structure intact
- Patch reflection ritual present
- No artificial escalation or resolution

## Rework Format

If sending back for rework, use this format:

```
REWORK (SE): [specific issue] | [why it violates style] | [desired effect]
```

Example:
```
REWORK (SE): Paragraph starting "Nexus explained the concept of..." | Nexus doesn't lecture/explain | Rewrite as question or metaphor that guides without teaching
```
